Random Hall is located on Massachusetts Avenue in Cambridge, just north of Memorial Drive. If you're coming from Route 93, take the Storrow Drive exit. Exit Storrow Drive at Mass Ave, and drive approximately one mile. Random Hall will be on the Left.

If you're using public transportation, take the MBTA's Red Line to Central Square and then walk south, towards MIT. Random Hall will be on the right, approximately four blocks from the stop. The Route 1 and CT1 buses pass directly in front of Random Hall, and have nearby stops.
