The Brady Bunch Dimension

Perhaps the most important part of Random is that we're a family. Now that means a lot of different things to different people, so let us qualify that. Random is a very open and trusting place. Most residents leave their doors open even when they're not in their room. Residents usually eat together in the kitchens and sometimes even cook real food together. Residents hang out together, and we genuinely care about each other.

If you've had a bad day, it is almost certain that someone will notice and ask. If you just figured out something really cool, there are tons of people around who'd love to listen. People here are just really close and take care of each other by working and playing together.

It's really about trust and caring. And isn't that what a family is all about?
